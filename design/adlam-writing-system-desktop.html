𞤀𞤁𞤂𞤃: The Gift of the
Barry Brothers

Created in the late 1980s in N'Zérékoré, Guinea, the ADLaM script was born from the vision of Ibrahima and Abdoulaye Barry. Observing that the Fulani people lacked a writing system specifically tailored to the phonetics of their language, Fulfulde, the young brothers spent years refining these unique characters.

"Alkule Dandayɗe Leñol Mulugol" — The alphabet that protects the people from vanishing.

Writing Direction
