Did you know?

The ADLaM script was created in the late 1980s by two teenage brothers, Ibrahima and Abdoulaye Barry, to preserve the Fulfulde language. Vowels play a crucial role in maintaining the rhythmic flow of the spoken language.

info

In ADLaM, vowels are written first, and consonants follow them in a right-to-left flow.
