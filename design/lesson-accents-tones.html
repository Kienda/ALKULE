ADLaM Accent & Tone Marks

The ADLaM script uses specific diacritics to indicate vowel length, tone, and stress. Mastering these is crucial for correct pronunciation in Fulfulde.

Usage in Context

Observe how the tone marks change the meaning of similar looking words. In ADLaM, the pitch is as important as the letters themselves.

Pro Tip

Tone marks are always placed directly above or below the vowel they modify, never between characters.
