Lesson 4: Mathematics

ADLaM Numerals

Discover how the ADLaM script handles numbers. From basic digits to complex mathematical operations, ADLaM follows a consistent Right-to-Left logic that honors the script's visual rhythm.

info

About ADLaM Numbers

ADLaM uses the decimal system. While the characters are unique, the logic of addition, subtraction, and higher-order math remains identical to global standards. **Crucially**, like the text, numbers are written and read from right to left.
