Mastering Punctuation

The Art of ADLaM Punctuation

In the ADLaM script, punctuation serves as the breath of the language. It governs the flow, rhythm, and clarity of Fulfulde prose. Unlike Latin scripts, ADLaM punctuation is designed to complement the unique visual structure of the alphabet, ensuring that questions, pauses, and exclamations are distinctly marked while maintaining the calligraphic elegance of the text.

info PRO TIP

Always remember that ADLaM is read from right to left. Punctuation marks like the question mark are mirrored to guide the eye back into the flow of the next sentence.
